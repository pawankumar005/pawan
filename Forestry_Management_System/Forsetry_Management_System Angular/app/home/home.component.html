Popular products

Teak Wood

Teak is used extensively in India to make doors and window frames, furniture, and columns and beams in old type houses. It is resistant to termite attacks and damage caused by other insects.

Pine Wood

Pine wood is widely used in high-value carpentry items such as furniture, window frames, panelling, floors, and roofing, and the resin of some species is an important source of turpentine.

Oak

Oak wood has been used as a hardwood timber for thousands of years.Modern uses for oak wood include homewares, flooring, wine barrels and firewood.
